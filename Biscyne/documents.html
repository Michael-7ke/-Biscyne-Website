Tips

Document Preparation Tips

1

Start Early

Begin gathering documents as soon as you decide to apply. Some documents take weeks to process.

2

Keep Originals Safe

Always keep your original documents in a safe place. Make copies for reference.

3

Check Validity

Ensure your passport has at least 6 months validity and enough blank pages.

4

Quality Photos

Get passport photos from a professional studio with white background.

5

Ask for Help

If you're unsure about any document, contact us via WhatsApp for guidance.
