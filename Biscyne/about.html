Your Partner for Gulf Employment

Biscyne is a Kenyan recruitment and training agency dedicated to connecting qualified workers with verified employers in the Middle East, including Dubai, Kuwait, and Saudi Arabia.

We work with both first-time Gulf workers and experienced Ex-Gulf candidates, ensuring fast processing and transparent communication every step of the way.
